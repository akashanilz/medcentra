Specialties at Medcentra

A multi-disciplinary team of doctors working together to diagnose, treat and prevent illness – with personalized care plans crafted around your lifestyle and goals.

Family Medicine

Primary care for every stage of life – from routine check-ups to chronic disease management and vaccinations.

  • Annual health check-ups
  • Diabetes & hypertension follow-up
  • Immunizations & travel advice
Internal Medicine

Expert evaluation and management of complex adult medical conditions with evidence-based protocols.

  • Cardio-metabolic risk assessment
  • Respiratory & endocrine disorders
  • Pre-operative clearance
Pediatrics & Neonatology

Child-friendly spaces and specialist pediatricians for newborns, toddlers and teens.

  • Growth & development tracking
  • Childhood vaccination schedule
  • Allergy & asthma care
Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Compassionate women’s health services covering routine care, pregnancy and advanced gynecological concerns.

  • Antenatal & postnatal care
  • PCOS & hormonal evaluation
  • Screening for cervical & breast health
Dental & Orthodontics

From routine cleaning to full-mouth rehabilitation and smile design with modern, pain-controlled techniques.

  • Scaling, fillings & root-canal treatment
  • Braces & aligners
  • Crowns, veneers & implants
Orthopedics & Sports Medicine

Comprehensive bone, joint and sports-injury care to get you back to pain-free movement.

  • Back & neck pain clinics
  • Joint injections & fracture care
  • Sports injury rehabilitation
Dermatology & Aesthetics

Medical and cosmetic dermatology for healthy, confident skin at every age.

  • Acne, pigmentation & hair fall
  • Anti-aging treatments
  • Laser & minimally invasive procedures
ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)

Specialist care for sinus, allergy and ENT conditions with endoscopic evaluation.

  • Sinusitis & allergy management
  • Ear infections & hearing issues
  • Throat & voice disorders
Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Goal-based recovery programs designed to restore mobility and strength safely.

  • Post-surgical rehab
  • Neck, back & joint pain therapy
  • Sports & work-related injuries
Laboratory & Radiology

Accurate and timely diagnostics with advanced equipment and strict quality checks.

  • Routine & advanced blood tests
  • Ultrasound & X-ray
  • Corporate & visa medicals
